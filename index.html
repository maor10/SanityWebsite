About

Sanity is a first person horror game developed in Unreal Engine 4. It's mission is to create the scariest, most gut wrenching game in existence.

Sanity is going to be the most horrifying game ever made, and we're always looking for new twists and ideas. Within a few weeks, we're going to be giving early access to forums for the game. Our developers and designers will be asking questions, looking for your opinion on every aspect of the game.
Our vision is that Sanity will be built by YOU.

Storyline

Victor Marvel is going to work, when a group, of what appear to be mentally deranged patients, break out from a bus transferring them to another asylum. Trapped in the building, with the electricity out, Victor must escape. But these patients, they are not just mentally deranged..

Development

Development is done in Unreal Engine 4, one of the newest most modern tools today. The game will be made for all PS4, Xbox One, PS3, Xbox 360, and of course: PC.



Design

The games design is perhaps the most exciting part- being done by one of the greatest 3D designers today, it may end up having the best graphics of any horror game.
